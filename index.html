What is Guitar Tracker?

Hey, you! You're a guitarist, right? Stop and think right now:
When was the last time you changed the strings on your guitar?
How about on that old acoustic you play around campfires in the summer?
When was the last time you had it properly set up?
If you're having trouble answering these questions, you've come to the right place. This webapp serves as a database for your guitars. Gone are the days of instrument abuse and neglect.
